Booking Information


To make a booking:  Please email sunnysalcombe@gmail.com to check availability.

OR proceed to our partner website:

http://www.homeaway.co.uk/p1052442

 

No smokers.
No pets without prior arrangement.
Parking: One car.

Car Park weekly tickets Available in person or through the post from Salcombe Tourist Information, Market Street, Salcombe TQ8 8DE. (NB space is not guaranteed.)
Please send cheque for £32 for 7 days parking, payable to Salcombe Information, dates & time of arrival & departure, car registration number, please include a SAE and they will send your car parking ticket return of post.

A cleaner can be provided for a thorough clean at the end of your stay for £35. Sheets can be provided at £5 per bed.  The cleaner will make the beds up and wash and return, plus £5 per washing load of towels.

Property is available at 12.00noon.

All bookings must be confirmed with a 20% deposit and cannot be held until this is received. 

Confirmation of your booking will be by email.

Cancellation
Should you need to cancel we will refund your deposit if you cancel one month before your arrival.

Bookings can be made for weeks, Friday to Friday and in the mid and low season, weekends and mid-week breaks.

To make a booking or to find out any further information please contact by email sunnysalcombe@gmail.com.
